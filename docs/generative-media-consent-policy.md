# Generative Media Consent Policy

This policy applies before any local music, audio, or video provider is promoted. Deterministic enforcement belongs outside model prompts and provider UIs.

## Required Disclosures

Before a network call, model download, reference-media read, or generated-artifact write, disclose the exact provider/model revision, license and commercial-use boundary, sources and network destinations, download and temporary-storage sizes, output location, expected runtime and hardware use, retained provider copies, cleanup, rollback, and uninstall behavior.

## Reference Media And Identity

- Reference audio, images, and video require explicit purpose-bound consent from the user controlling the files.
- Voice cloning, recognizable voices, likeness, face animation, lip synchronization, and identity transfer require an affirmative rights confirmation and a separate effect-bound approval.
- The system must reject attempts to impersonate a real person deceptively, bypass consent, or conceal material synthetic manipulation.
- Reference paths, biometric or identity-bearing inputs, prompts, lyrics, and credentials remain local and are not persisted in evidence.

## Creative Rights And Disclosure

- Lyrics and other supplied text require the user to confirm appropriate rights or a lawful basis for use.
- Artist-style requests must not be represented as endorsement, collaboration, or authentic output by that artist.
- Provider/model attribution and license notices must be retained when required.
- Commercial-use status is derived from the exact model and dependency licenses, not from a generic provider claim.
- Generated-content disclosure must be available and becomes mandatory for identity-bearing, deceptive-context, or externally published media where policy or law requires it.

## Storage And Retention

The user selects or approves the output destination. Provider history, temporary media, intermediate frames, reference copies, and failed outputs have explicit retention limits and cleanup controls. Ordinary engine updates and uninstalls do not delete user-owned artifacts. Cleanup is previewed and separately approved.

## Promotion Gate

Instrumental and vocal audio, text-to-video and image-to-video, and every operating-system/accelerator profile are independent evidence cells. A passing operation never promotes another operation or hardware path. Failed or incomplete candidates leave sanitized documentation only and add no scripts, adapters, harnesses, templates, workflows, configuration, runtime files, or active registry entries.
